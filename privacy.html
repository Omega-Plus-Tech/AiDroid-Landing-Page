Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 17, 2026

AiDroid LLP ("AiDroid", "we", "us", "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect through this website, how we use it, and the choices you have.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you voluntarily provide when you fill out the contact / inquiry form on this website, including:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • The service you're interested in and project details you share with us

We do not knowingly collect sensitive personal information (such as payment card details, government ID numbers, or health data) through this website.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your inquiry and provide a project quotation or estimate
  • To communicate with you about your project via email, phone, or WhatsApp
  • To maintain internal records of inquiries and engagements
  • To improve our services and website

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

3. How Form Submissions Are Processed

The contact form on this website is processed using FormSubmit (formsubmit.co), a third-party form-processing service that securely delivers your submission to our email inbox at support@aidroid.co.in. We do not store form submissions in our own database — they are received directly via email.

4. Third-Party Services Used on This Site

  • FormSubmit — processes and delivers contact form submissions via email.
  • Google Fonts — used to load website typography.
  • Font Awesome (cdnjs) — used to load icons on the website.

These third-party providers may receive standard technical information (such as IP address and browser type) as part of normal web requests, governed by their own respective privacy policies.

5. Cookies

This website does not currently use tracking or advertising cookies. If we add analytics or marketing tools in the future, this policy will be updated to reflect that, along with options to manage your preferences where applicable.

6. Data Retention

We retain inquiry information for as long as necessary to respond to your request, manage an ongoing engagement, or comply with legal/accounting obligations. You may request deletion of your information at any time as described below.

7. Your Rights

You may contact us at any time to:

  • Request a copy of the information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information from our records
  • Withdraw consent to be contacted

To exercise any of these rights, email us at support@aidroid.co.in.

8. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the information shared with us. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Children's Privacy

This website is intended for businesses and individuals seeking software development services and is not directed at children under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your information is handled, please contact us at:
